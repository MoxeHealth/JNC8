This application is a high blood pressure (hypertension) treatment calculator and visualizer based on the JNC8 guidelines. It's intended to be an interactive educational experience through which a doctor can lead his or her patient during a visit.

The development of the application was sponsored by Moxe Health, a Rock Health company. The application was primarily developed on top of Moxe Health's EHR integration service, Substrate, which allowed us to write software for a single, simple data structure and have it accept any format of EHR.
